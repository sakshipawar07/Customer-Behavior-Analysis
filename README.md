# 📊 Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard

A comprehensive **Power BI dashboard** developed to analyze customer purchasing behavior and shopping trends. The dashboard provides valuable insights into customer demographics, sales performance, product preferences, subscription behavior, and payment methods, enabling data-driven business decisions.

## 📊 Key Features
- Executive Dashboard
- Customer Demographics Analysis
- Sales & Purchase Analytics
- Product Category Analysis
- Customer Behavior Analysis

### 📌 Executive Dashboard
Provides an overview of key business metrics, including total customers, total sales, average purchase value, average customer rating, and subscription rate.

### 📌 Customer & Sales Analytics
Analyzes customer demographics, purchasing patterns, sales by category, seasonal sales trends, and payment method preferences to understand customer behavior.

### 📌 Product & Purchase Analysis
Provides insights into product category performance, purchase frequency, discount usage, previous purchases, and customer ratings.

## 🎯 Project Objective
To build an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard that transforms raw customer data into meaningful insights, helping businesses understand customer behavior, improve sales strategies, and support data-driven decision-making.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- SQL
- Python
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization

## 💡 Key Outcome
The dashboard consolidates customer and sales data into a single interactive reporting solution, allowing users to monitor KPIs, analyze purchasing trends, identify customer preferences, and make informed business decisions through dynamic visualizations and filters.
